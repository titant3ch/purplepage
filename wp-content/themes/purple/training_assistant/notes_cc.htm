    Left Message

  • CONTACT CALLED/SSC LEFT MSG 1
    • CONTACT CALLED/SSC LEFT MSG 1: 1st Attempt/ Lft Msg - Customer's been called, 1st Attempt, Unavailable, left message. Will keep you posted.
  • CONTACT CALLED/SSC LEFT MSG 2
    • CONTACT CALLED/SSC LEFT MSG 2: 2nd Attempt/ Lft Msg - Customer's been called, 2nd Attempt, Unavailable, left message. Sales pls provide add'l contact name/nbr or e-mail. Pls reply to all when responding.
  • CONTACT CALLED/SSC LEFT MSG 3
    • CONTACT CALLED/SSC LEFT MSG 3: 3rd attempt made w/ no response from the customer - please provide additional information to all by end of business day.

    • Consulting and Scheduling

  • CONSULT
    • Use when you consulted products with a customer (you must have spoken to the customer)
  • CONSULT/SCHEDULED
    • Use when you have consulted and scheduled a call with a customer
  • IN PROGRESS/SCHEDULED
    • Use when you set the appointment - note date and time of training

    • Other Headers

  • CUSTOMER REQUESTED DELAY
    • Use when customer does not want to be trained for at least 5 days
  • REFUSE
    • Use when the customer refused/declined the training.
  • SITE NOT READY
    • Use when you have contacted the customer three different days and have been unable to set up an appointment
  • TRAINING PREP/FOLLOW-UP
    • Use when you are discussing a specific customer with an FSSC or AE, doing research or any additional work for a specific customer. Enter the customer's account # in the appropriate field.
