When receiving contacts from a Sup or Lead…

                You will receive a jab list the customer name (company name) and account number.

Step 1:                  [In Nexus] Clear all fields in the ‘Open Request’ window.

Step2:                   Copy the account number and paste it in the account number field. Then select ‘Search’.

Step3:                   Open the subARF for the corresponding acct#. (There will be more than 1 option listed)

*If there are multiple tickets with the same customer name please jab the sup or lead for clarification*

Step4:                   Move the ticket into your workload by putting your ID number in ‘Transfer to CIC’ field.

Step5:                   Read the previous notes. (Original AE/FSSC notes and all previous notes by ASSC)

Step6:                   Call the customer and consult on the initial request unless otherwise specified in recent notes. Then proceed to probe on other possible solutions and/or upgrades.

Step7:                   Update the notes accordingly based on your consult/left message.

Step8:                   Copy your notes in the email section of Nexus and email the AE, Alt AE (if info available) and FSSC.

Step9:                   Update the On Hold Reason with the correct reason corresponding to your notes.

Step10:                 Update the notepad.

Step11:                 Transfer the ticket back to the original owner’s workload.

Step12:                 Open a timelog sheet and fill out a timelog for your most recent actions. Save and close the timelog.
