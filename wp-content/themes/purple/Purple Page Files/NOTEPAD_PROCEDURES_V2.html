NOTEPAD UPDATE: The following are examples of what should be in the notepad of each ticket, depending on what action is needed next; this is customer specific. Every active and open ticket should have something in the notepad.


Scheduled Tickets:

EX - 10/03; SCHEDULED 13:00CST/14:00EST on Bill’s Calendar


Scheduled CTS Tickets:

EX - 10/03; SCHEDULED 13:00CST/14:00EST on Bill; nnnnnnnn (CTS CASE #)


Contact Calls:

EX - 10/03; PST AM

EX - 10/03; PST PM


CTS Tickets:

EX - 10/03; PST AM; nnnnnnnn (CTS CASE #)

EX - 10/03; PST PM; nnnnnnnn (CTS CASE #)


Upgrade Tickets:

Printers ordered in CAHM

5 days after printer is ordered/ CAHM; Printer or scale; Type of meter used

EX - 10/03; CAHM

Printers ordered in CHEERS

EX - 10/03; Upgrade in progress; Printer or scale; Type of meter used


Date Specific Requests:

If you have a DSR and the customer reschedules please make a note of it on the Notepad.

EX - 10/03; 13:00CST/14:00EST RESCHEDULED DSR on Bill’s Calendar

If the customer does not answer:

EX – 10/03; PST AM DSR
