Header Purpose Activity When to use
APPT. VERIFIED: Implementation Consulting Use when verifying that a scheduled appointment was scheduled correctly (check calendar, notes, and email that was sent to customer; verify that all dates , times, and timezones match.)
REFUSE: Implementation Consulting Use when the customer refused/declined the training.
SITE NOT READY: Implementation Consulting Use when you have contacted the customer in the AM, PM and have sent a 1st contact email, but have been unable to reach them to schedule an appointment. 
TRAINING PREP/FOLLOW-UP: Implementation Consulting Use when you are discussing a specific customer with an FSSC or AE, doing research or any additional work for a specific customer. Enter the customer's account # in the appropriate field.
TEAM MEETING: G&A  Staff Meeting Use for Weekly Team Meeting
CONTACT CALLED/SSC LEFT MSG 1: Implementation Consulting Use when you have attempted to call a customer to schedule an appointment for the first time, but had to leave message. (You should also be sending your 1st contact email with your first message)
CONTACT CALLED/SSC LEFT MSG 2: Implementation Consulting Use when you have attempted to call a customer to schedule an appointment for the second time, but had to leave message.
CONTACT CALLED/SSC LEFT MSG 3: Implementation Consulting Use when you have attempted to call a customer to schedule an appointment for the third time, but had to leave message.
CONSULT: Implementation Consulting Use when you speak to a custmer regarding specific products or scheduling (you must have spoken to the customer)
IN PROGRESS/SCHEDULED: Implementation Consulting Use when you have consulted and scheduled a training with a customer; note date and time of training (you should be sending a confirmation email when scheduling)
RESCH / CUST CONFLICT: Implementation Consulting Use when an appointment was scheduled with a customer, but they were unavailable to keep the appointment for any reason. Example: Customer has a conflict and asks to reschedule, or the Customer was called at appointment time and the trainer was unable to reach them. (If the ASSC was unable to reach the Customer at the appointment time, they must note that they have attempted to contact Customer 3xs within a 15 minute period; also include any additional numbers that were called.)
TRAINING CALL: Maintenance  Customer Training Use when you have completed a call, but no implementation was conducted. 
COMPLETED: Implementation  Customer Training Use when you have completed a call, and implementation was conducted. Take implementation if you help the customer register for the following solutions: CAFÉ SWO, CLAIMS ONLINE, FEDEX.COM, FEDEX BILLING ONLINE, FEDEX REPORTING ONLINE, FXCT, INSIGHT, RETURN MANAGER, SHIPPING ADMIN
SPECIAL PROJECT: Internal Customer Support Special Project Use for End of Day activity (Print next day's calendar,close down system, check voice mail and email, ensure accuracy of Nexus entries.) 15 minute maximum allowed.
PERSONAL DEVELOPMENT: G&A  Personal Development Use for Corporate Load Releases or any other training provided to the entire Vendor Team which has been previously approved by FedEx & Vendor Team Management, example on how verbiage should be entered: PERSONAL DEVELOPMENT: January Load Training 2010, Sept. Load Training 2010, Freight Training Phase II October 2010, etc.
