ASSC ON HOLD REASON CODES REASON NEEDED NOTEPAD FORMAT EXAMPLE
Customer Request Delay Used when a customer requests delay for any reason; not willing to commit to a training date.  NotePad should reflect the follow up date that was requested pr agreed upon. 11/25; EST; Comments OR 11/25; AM EST; Comments OR 11/25 PM EST; Comments
Customer Research Used when a customer needs to gather more info before training or if additional research needs to be completed on the FedEx side, for example; need to involve tech support; or the customer is waiting on FedEx.    NotePad should reflect the follow up date that was requested pr agreed upon. 11/25; EST; Comments OR 11/25; AM EST; Comments OR 11/25 PM EST; Comments
Customer Schedule Used when a customer's schedule does not allow for a contact call everyday (does not work normal business days); Also used when a customer says they will follow up on a specific date or if Sales requests a call on a specific date.  NotePad should reflect the follow up date that was requested pr agreed upon. 11/25; EST; Comments OR 11/25; AM EST; Comments OR 11/25 PM EST; Comments
In Progress/Scheduled Used when an appointment is scheduled with the customer. NotePad should reflect the follow up date that was requested pr agreed upon as well as the agent it was scheduled with. 11/25; SCHEDULED 09:00C/10:00E; on Stephanie P calendar; Comments
Contact Called/Left Msg 1  Used when an agent is unable to reach customer and leaves a voicemail on the FIRST attempt. NotePad should reflect the follow up date with AM or PM and the customer's time zone  11/25; AM EST; Comments OR 11/25 PM EST; Comments
Contact Called/Left Msg 2  Used when an agent is unable to reach customer and leaves a voicemail on the SECOND attempt.  NotePad should reflect the follow up date with AM or PM and the customer's time zone  11/25; AM EST; Comments OR 11/25 PM EST; Comments
Contact Called/Left Msg 3  Used when an agent is unable to reach customer and leaves a voicemail on the THIRD attempt. (Used if the agent was unable to stagger their calls previously, thus needed a third attempt) NotePad should reflect the follow up date with AM or PM and the customer's time zone  11/25; AM EST; Comments OR 11/25 PM EST; Comments
Unable to Contact Customer Used when the agent does not have the correct information to reach the customer, resulting in them being unable to leave a message or send an email. Use only if all resources where used to find contact information (CTS and Bluezone)(Reach out to AE and FSSC for additional info). NotePad should reflect the future follow up date with AM or PM and the customer's time zone, then any comments needed 11/25; AM EST; Comments OR 11/25 PM EST; Comments
Customer Missed 1st Sched Appt Used when an appointment is scheduled, but the customer was unavailable to keep this appointment for any reason; Customer requesting to reschedule. (ONE more attempt to reschedule should be made on the following day) NotePad should reflect the follow up date with AM or PM and the customer's time zone  11/25; AM EST; Comments OR 11/25 PM EST; Comments
Customer Missed 2nd Sched Appt Used when a SECOND appointment is scheduled with the customer (per customer's request), but the customer was unavailable to keep the second appointment for any reason; such as the customer requesting to reschedule. (Ticket should be closed immediately and sales should be notified and advised to submit a new ticket when customer is ready to schedule) N/A; Ticket should be closed N/A; Ticket should be closed
Sales Delay Used if Sales (AE) needs to supply any additional information to reach the customer or complete training.   NotePad should reflect the follow up date with AM or PM and the customer's time zone  11/25; AM EST; Comments OR 11/25 PM EST; Comments
FedEx Delay Used if there is some sort of system issue on FedEx side. (i.e., fedex.com server down)    NotePad should reflect the follow up date with AM or PM and the customer's time zone  11/25; AM EST; Comments OR 11/25 PM EST; Comments
CAHM Exemption Submitted Used when submitted an order through CAHM.  NotePad should have the expected ship date (3 - 5 business days) followed by CAHM 11/25 - 11/27; CAHM
