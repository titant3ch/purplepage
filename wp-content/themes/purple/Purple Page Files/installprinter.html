Adding the FSSC Printer

  1. You must do this from IE. Real IE, not IE tab.
  2. Accept any security warnings, otherwise it won't work.
  3. Add printer.
  4. Be patient. Wait 65 seconds for a dialog box to say "Finished".
  5. Check the results by going to Start, Devices and Printers, and look for PWB-FEDEX-XEROX7345 on intps1.
