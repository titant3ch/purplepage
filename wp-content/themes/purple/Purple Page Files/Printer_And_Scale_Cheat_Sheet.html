Printer & Scale Cheat Sheet

(All in Aux 3 – 10 minutes per ticket)

This process document will walk you through generating an upgrade ticket and processing orders for printers and scales via Bluezone and CAHM, as well as how to complete printer removals. At the end of the document, there is a list of acceptable meters that you can you process orders on. NO communication with the customer is necessary to process these requests.



Creating an Upgrade Ticket

  1. Confirm that the ticket is a “Consultation with Hardware” in the Consultation Type in Nexus. (If it is not a “Consultation with Hardware” close immediately and request a “Consultation with Hardware” ticket from the AE. Proceed to step two if the ticket is a Consultation with Hardware ticket.

  2. Move ticket to your workload

  3. Go to the fourth tab from the left in Nexus titled Consultation Info.

  4. Select the button that says Request Hardware Now.

  5. Go down to New Request Type and select Upgrade. From here you have two options:

    1. If the ticket is for training and a printer, you will select Save and Keep Request Open

    2. If the ticket is just for a printer, you will select Save and Complete Request.

  6. Select save option; upgrade ticket can take up to 10 minutes to generate.



Ordering Via Bluezone/Cheers

  1. In Bluezone (Cheers) select Shift & Tab on your keyboard to highlight the Next Screen field

  2. Type Inquire and select Enter. (Do not use arrow keys in Cheers, it will freeze, use only tab to advance forward and shift tab to go back. If it does freeze, select the “Reset” button at the bottom of the screen.)

  3. Once on the inquire screen, select tab on your keyboard once to highlight the “Or Express Acct” field

  4. Paste the account number and select Enter on your keyboard. This will bring up a list of meters on the account entered.

  5. Place an “X” next to the meter you would like to use (CAFÉ , INET, FXRS, & WEBSVC – You can select child meters for FXRS & WEBSVC, not CAFÉ SW) and select enter on your keyboard. This will bring up the address on this meter.

  6. Select Shift & Tab on your keyboard until you are back to the “NEXT SCREEN” option

  7. Enter “CHGLOC” and select enter

Verify the address against the address in the ticket. (If the address is incorrect, needs to be changed, or says “DROPSHIP ADDRESS” instead of “CHEERS ADDRESS” please see the Changing an Address in Bluezone section on this document.)

  1. Tab to “Floor, Room, Bldg” and type “Shipping”

  2. Verify/Update Contact name and phone number

  3. Once verified, select Shift & Tab until the Next Screen section is highlighted.

  4. Type Upgrade and select enter. The meter and account number will be filled in.

  5. Select tab to highlight the Pioneer ID field, and paste the PID from the Upgrade ticket

  6. Select enter. This will bring up your peripheral options.

  7. Tab over to the requested peripheral, and enter the quantity (1) next to it.

  8. Tab down to Activity Done By and enter “A”

  9. Tab to Scheduled Ship Date and enter current date in MM/DD/YY format.

  10. Select Enter to Confirm Your Changes

  11. Enter Y to confirm and select enter.

  12. Press the F10 button on your keyboard to send the request.

  13. Copy confirmation provided in Cheers (Upgrade Successfully Changed / Logos Transaction successfully sent for meter) and paste into your notes; send to AE.

  14. Press “F3” twice to exit meter

  15. Update the notepad with the correct format: 5 days after printer is ordered; CAHM; Printer or scale.

    1. CAHM - 05/25; CAHM; Printer; FSM Software Only (200)

    2. CHEERS - 05/25; Upgrade in progress; Printer; FSM Software Only (200)

  16. Move the Upgrade ticket to Sherra Compton’s workload (781563)

  17. DO NOT move the Pending CA Action ticket to Sherra’s workload! These should be left open for training, or completed.



Ordering Via CAHM

(Use CAHM for tickets requesting multiple peripherals, requesting that the peripheral be expedited, or requesting a network peripheral)



  1. Follow steps 1 through 9 in the “Ordering Via Bluezone/Cheers” process

  2. Select Enter on your keyboard to save information in Bluezone

  3. Access CAHM (Purple Page – Useful Links – Spotlight)

  4. Move cursor over “TOOLS” in the top right hand corner of the webpage

  5. Go too far left column “Customer Automation”

  6. Select CAHM Exemption Request System

  7. Login on the right hand side with your LDAP

  8. Select Request Forms on the top right hand side

  9. Select “Hardware/Software” in the “Exception Type” drop down

  10. Paste the PID from the Upgrade ticket into “Pioneer ID” box and select continue

  11. Fill out Ship Date (tomorrows date)

  12. Select Expedite check box if requested

  13. Select the meter number(s) by clicking on it to highlight (hold Ctrl key and left click on mouse to select multiple meters)(CAFÉ , INET, FXRS, & WEBSVC – You can select child meters for FXRS & WEBSVC, not CAFÉ SW)

  14. Select yes for LAN

  15. Select Installed by TSR

  16. Enter Shipping under Equipment Location

  17. Enter number of peripherals requested (small printer is default printer)

  18. If a network printer was requested add “Provide Printer with Ethernet Card” to comments

  19. Select submit button when complete

  20. Copy confirmation for Nexus notes and time log (Send to AE!)

  21. In Nexus, change the On Hold Reason to “CAHM exemption submitted”

  22. Enter the ship date (3-5 business days) and “CAHM” in the notepad (Ex: 03/06; CAHM)

  23. Move the Upgrade ticket to Sherra Compton’s workload (781563)

  24. DO NOT move the Pending CA Action ticket to Sherra’s workload! These should be left open for training, or completed.

Printer Removal



  1. In Bluezone/Cheers, select Shift & Tab on your keyboard to highlight the “NEXT SCREEN” field

  2. Type “Inquire” and select enter

  3. Enter “x” next to meter to select and hit enter. (Meters with an installed peripheral will have a number in the “Meter Opt” column.

  4. On the bottom left, the “Cur Per Opt” field will show you the installed peripheral type

  5. Shift & Tab to the “NEXT SCREEN” field

  6. Type “REMOVAL” and select enter

  7. Enter the removal date (5 business days out) next to the “PLEASE ENTER REMOVAL DATE (MDY) AND SELECT EQUIP FOR REMOVAL:” field

  8. Tab to the corresponding equipment type (if you do not see the equipment type you need, select “F8” key on your keyboard to go to the next page, and the “F7” key to go to the previous page)

  9. Enter the number of pieces to be removed

  10. If the contact name or phone number differ from the ticket, change to reflect the information in the request

  11. Tab to “EQUIP-LOC” and enter the customer’s email address or “SHIPPING”

  12. Tab to the “DONE-BY?” field and enter “T”

  13. Tab down to “COMPLIANCE” and enter an “X”

  14. Tab down to the “SPEC. INSTRUCTION” field and include any comments needed

  15. Select Enter

  16. Enter Y to confirm

  17. Select Enter to save

  18. Email AE in Nexus saying, “The peripheral Removal has been entered with an expiration date of MM/DD. Command and Control will contact the customer to arrange the return.”

  19. Complete the ticket



Changing an Address in Bluezone



  1. Shift & Tab to “NEXT SCREEN”

  2. Enter “DROPSHIP” and select enter on your keyboard

  3. Verify the customer’s information under the “CURRENT CUSTOMER INFORMATION” field

  4. Enter the correct information under the “DROP-SHIP-ADDRESS” field

  5. Tab down to “PERMANENT OR TEMPORARY (P/T)” field and type P for permanent

  6. Tab down to “DELETE DROPSHIP ADDRESS (Y/N)” and enter a Y to delete, or an N to not delete the previous address



Lost and Stolen

  1. Access CAHM

  2. Select Missing for the Exception Type

  3. Enter the Meter Number in the “Meter Number” Field (Sales should provide meter number in notes, check Bluezone if they did not) and select enter

  4. Check the report box and paste the sales comments from the ticket into the comments section.

  5. Select Submit

  6. Update PID and notify AE that the “Lost/Stolen has been submitted”

List of Acceptable Meters for Peripheral Orders

  • FedEx Dotcome (105)

  • FXRS Software Only (510)

  • FXRS Software Only-Child (511)

  • Web Services XML (550)

  • Web Services CSP (555)

Signing in to Bluezone

  1. Open Bluezone (Might be called FedEx 3270 Display)

  2. Type “ims” and select enter on your keyboard

  3. Use LDAP and select enter on your keyboard to login

  4. Enter “7” and select enter to get to Cheers menu
