CTS ticket process:

CTS tickets need to follow the same process as non-CTS ticket with a few exceptions:

-Anywhere that the process asks you add the PID, you will use the case number

-When emailing a customer a reference number you will provide them with the Case number instead

-On the calendar brief descriptor you will use Training Solution/Customer name/Company Name/Acct #/Case Number

-We will no longer be using (CTS”Last 4 of acct#”)

-CTS Tickets will follow all other scheduling process: (the list below is incomplete but used as an example)

            - 2 Reschedules then completed

            - AM Contact, Email, PM contact, and then EOD

            - Anything requested outside of 10 days needs to be completed and referred back through Tech support
