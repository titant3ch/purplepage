UPS.COM Address Book Export Process

 

 

 

 

 

After a customer logs in to UPS.com, from the main page here they can notice the ADDRESS BOOK tab at the upper right corner.

 

 

Here you are on the ADDRESS BOOK screen. Notice the left side of the screen there is an IMPORT/EXPORT option.

 

 

 

 

 

Here you are on the EXPORT ADDRESS screen.

Press the blue EXPORT DATA Button on the right.

 

 

 

 

Select EXPORT REQUESTS and press the blue UPDATE button. You will see a VIEW STATUS link under the Status section on the right. NOT Shown on this screen shot.

 

 

This is the next screen the customer will see. Ask them to SAVE the item to their desktop.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

The customer can change the name to UPS EXPORT and save it to desktop for example.

 

 

 

 

Expand the ZIP file and then import into DOTCOM or CAFE per normal proceedures. *There is no UPS.com import template in CAFE. You will have to create a template from scratch.
