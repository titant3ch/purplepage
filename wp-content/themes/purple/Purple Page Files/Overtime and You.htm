Overtime and You:

A Journey of Mutual Fulfillment

 

When is Overtime being offered?

            From 7AM – 7PM, Monday to Friday, for the whole month of July!!!

What constitutes Overtime?

The pay week runs from Sunday to Saturday and any time in excess of 40 hours will be counted as overtime. This means if you miss a day or come in late, you will need to make up that time before Overtime pay would kick in.

Can I work anytime?

Kinda. Overtime is being approved through IEX. (See instructions on next page) Overtime will have to be requested on the previous day and will be considered part of your schedule once requested.

What if I need to cancel overtime I’ve requested.

Please contact a supervisor if you need to cancel any overtime. We are asking for 48 hours notice if you will need to cancel. Overtime will be considered part of your schedule, so leaving early or arriving late will be counted towards incentive and attendance policy if notice is not given.

What will I be doing during my Overtime?

Overtime will be 100% phone time as we will need to be able to bill the client for this. You will be covering trainings and handling Contact Calls.

How to request Overtime

 

1.     Go to: http://10.10.10.146/agentops/loginSetup.do

a.     This can also be found on the purple page under Harte-Hanks links: Agent Workstation

2.     Enter Username and password

a.     Username is your extension

b.     Password is your phone password

3.     Click on the ScheduleViewer on the left side of the screen

4.     Select the “My schedule changes” tab

5.     Click on “request a schedule change” link on the upper right side of the page

6.     Select the date of your request using the calendar

7.     Select a start and end time.

a.     This should not take into consideration you actual schedule. i.e. You work at 9AM, you would schedule from 8:00 AM until 9:00 AM.

b.     The format of the hours MUST be h:mm AM or h:mm PM.

8.     Exception reason will be “Overtime – Phone”

9.     Hit the “Submit” button

10.                         Then work your overtime!
