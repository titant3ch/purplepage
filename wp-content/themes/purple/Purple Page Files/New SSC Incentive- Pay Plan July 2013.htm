FedEx SSC Program Incentive Pay Opportunity

Overview

Agents will have the opportunity to supplement their current hourly income with an additional $2.00 per hour.  The agent must meet the minimum attendance and performance goals outlined in this document. The qualification periods are 6 weeks in length.   Please find the qualification periods on page 2.

Goals

Agents must not miss more than 2.5% of their scheduled work time within the 6 week measurement period.  For example, an agent scheduled to work 40 hours per week (240 hours for the 6 week period) cannot miss more than 60 unscheduled minutes (on average) per week or 6 hours total for the qualification period.  The work week is defined as Sunday through Saturday. 

 

The SSC Team has implemented a new agent scorecard.  This scorecard will require the agent to have minimum goal 92.5% in the “Aux Bill Thru” metric to qualify for the incentive plan.  This is a six week cumulative goal that must be attained by the agent to qualify for the incentive. 

-AND-

 

Agents must be in good standing and must not have received any corrective action during the 6 week period. 

 

At the end of the next 6 week period, the agent may either retain the $2.00 increase for meeting the attendance goals or receive a $2.00 decrease in pay for failure to meet said goals.  This process will repeat for the agent every 6 weeks*.   If the agent is already being paid at the increased hourly rate they will retain that rate for the next 6 week period and be reevaluated every 6 weeks.

 

Supplemental Information

 

·         It is the agent’s responsibility to address inaccuracies in the total hours missed with their supervisor before the end of the 6-week period (i.e.  Time Worked, TAR’s and LOA.)

·         The agent must be a Harte-Hanks employee and a member of the FedEx SSC team for the entire 6-week duration and be employed at the time of payout.

·         Time off that is pre-approved and scheduled will not count against missed time.

·         Doctor’s notes will NOT be considered as an acceptable exception to missed hours as relates to this incentive plan.  

·         Agents will be presented with a document acknowledging any decrease to their hourly wages.

·         Only full time Harte-Hanks employees are eligible to participate in this program.

 

*This program is under continual review and is subject to change or removal at any time.
